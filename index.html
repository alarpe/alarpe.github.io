Research Interests

Chenkang is a Ph.D. candidate in fluid mechanics at CSSRC, specializing in computational fluid dynamics, machine learning, and high-performance computing and their applications in marine engineering. His research focuses on developing machine learning-based strategies for optimizing parallel computing resources in high-fidelity simulations .With a strong background in computational fluid dynamics and high-performance computing ,He has contributed to improving the efficiency and accuracy of simulations used in naval architecture. He has published in peer-reviewed journals , emphasizing innovative approaches to computational resource management and the integration of machine learning techniques in engineering simulations.
